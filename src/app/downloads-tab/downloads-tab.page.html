How Downloads Work

Some download buttons will take you to an external site to get the game. Some downloads come in multiple parts. Download all parts, put them in the same folder, and extract Part 1 — this will automatically combine everything into the full game file Once the download is complete, come back here and use the Setup Automation page to extract files if you did not extract them your self and move them where they need to go.

If the download starts directly, it will be handled by the app and placed in the proper folder automatically.
